3 nights, 4 days in Copenhagen

When Copenhagen was awarded the title of European Green Capital in 2014, the jury singled out the city’s urban planning and design. The city is also something of a transport pioneer, aiming to become the world’s most cycling-friendly city; a project that seemingly has total buy-in from Copenhagen folk.

Things to do in Copenhagen:

    3. Tivoli Gardens also offers many more relaxed rides for the little ones and for those who don’t want to feel their stomach turn. Watch fairytale scenes as you never have before in the Flying Trunk or climb on the back of an elephant at The Classic Carousel.
